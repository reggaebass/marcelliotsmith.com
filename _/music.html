MUSIC

All musicians are subconsciously mathematicians.
                            Thelonious Monk

I have been a fixture in the Boston music scene for over fifteen years. During that time I have had the pleasure of working with a multitude of fine musicians including Mighty Mystic, The Koga Ninjazz, The Mike Nolan Band, Stepper's Heaven, and the crew at Split2nd Entertainment. I have also had the opportunity to share a stage with some real legends. These include Barrington Levy, Steel Pulse, Freddy McGregor and The Wailers. I play a five string peavey millennium bxp bass paired with a Markbass combo and cabinet stack. With these tools I can bring the thump to any stage.
